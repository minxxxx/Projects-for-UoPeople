Unit 7: Time and Stress Management

Topics:

Personal time management methods.
Goal-setting.
Stress management techniques.

Learning Objectives:

By the end of this Unit, you will be able to:

Analyze your time use and prioritization.
Apply methods for personal time management to become more efficient and self-motivated.
Formulate realistic and achievable goals.
Apply stress management strategies to effectively manage personal stress.
Recognize and prepare basic APA citations and references.
