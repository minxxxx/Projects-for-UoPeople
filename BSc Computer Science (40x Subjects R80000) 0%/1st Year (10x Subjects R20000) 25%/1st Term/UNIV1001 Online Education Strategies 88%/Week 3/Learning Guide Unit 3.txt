Topics:

Research and writing with academic integrity.
Defining and explaining plagiarism.
APA format.
Netiquette.

Learning Objectives:

By the end of this Unit, you will be able to:

Examine plagiarism and provide specific methods for avoiding it.
Evaluate a case study for plagiarism issues.
Reflect on netiquette as it relates to peer assessment.
Recognize and prepare basic APA citations and references.
