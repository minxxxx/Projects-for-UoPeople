Overview
Unit 4: What is Critical Thinking and Why is it Important?

Topics:

Definition of critical thinking.
Explanation of the importance of critical thinking.
Methods for developing critical thinking skills and critical problem solving.
APA format.

Learning Objectives:

By the end of this Unit, you will be able to:

Explain why it is important to be able to think critically academically, as well as in various situations.
Identify at least 3 methods for critical thinking analysis.
Apply a critical thinking method for further depth of analysis in problem-solving.
Recognize and prepare basic APA citations and references.
